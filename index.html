About

  Jesse's Guitar Lessons is my submission for my final project in CGS1821. This website uses a traditional blog-style format with the ability to scale to any amount of posts. The header shows the 4 key areas that posts would fall under.

   The first area, blog, will feature opinion articles about musicians and their music.

   The second area, lessons, would feature posts teaching users how to play guitar.

   The third area, theory, would feature posts teaching music theory concepts such as scales, keys, rhythm, and more.

   Lastly, the reference area would feature quick guides for both guitar players and musicians in general. This would include downloadable PDFs on subjects such as chord shapes, scale shapes, and more.

   In all, I am very proud of the potential this website holds and its possible applications in the real world.
